In this article, I will talk about 
How to disable and enable macros in Microsoft Office.

------------------------------------------
Step 1:
File->Option->Trust Center
Step 2: 
Click "Trust Center Settings" button.
Step 3:
Select the option you want. Then click "ok" button.

What options are there? See the following link.
